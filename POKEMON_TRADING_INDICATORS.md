# Pokemon Card Price Index - Trading Indicators Guide

## Overview

The Pokemon Price Index now includes multiple momentum indicators to help identify optimal buy and sell opportunities.

## Available Indicators

### Moving Averages

1. **7-Day MA (Short-term Momentum)**
   - Captures recent price movements
   - More sensitive to price changes than longer MAs
   - **Buy Signal**: Price crosses above 7-day MA (bullish momentum)
   - **Sell Signal**: Price crosses below 7-day MA (bearish momentum)

2. **30-Day MA (Medium-term Trend)**
   - Balances responsiveness with trend stability
   - **Buy Signal**: Price above 30-day MA with upward slope
   - **Sell Signal**: Price below 30-day MA with downward slope

3. **120-Day MA (Long-term Trend)**
   - Represents the overall market trend
   - **Buy Signal**: Price above 120-day MA (bullish market)
   - **Sell Signal**: Price below 120-day MA (bearish market)

### MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

**Components:**
- **MACD Line**: 12-day EMA - 26-day EMA
- **Signal Line**: 9-day EMA of MACD
- **Histogram**: MACD - Signal (shows momentum strength)

**Buy Signals:**
- MACD crosses above Signal line (bullish crossover)
- MACD histogram turns positive (momentum increasing)
- MACD above zero (bullish momentum)

**Sell Signals:**
- MACD crosses below Signal line (bearish crossover)
- MACD histogram turns negative (momentum decreasing)
- MACD below zero (bearish momentum)

### Rate of Change (ROC)

**7-Day ROC**: Percentage change over 7 days
- **Buy Signal**: Positive and increasing (accelerating upward momentum)
- **Sell Signal**: Negative and decreasing (accelerating downward momentum)

**30-Day ROC**: Percentage change over 30 days
- Shows medium-term momentum
- **Buy Signal**: Positive ROC above 5% (strong upward trend)
- **Sell Signal**: Negative ROC below -5% (strong downward trend)

## Trading Strategy Recommendations

### Conservative Approach (Long-term Holders)
1. **Buy when:**
   - Price is above 120-day MA
   - 30-day MA is above 120-day MA (golden cross)
   - MACD is positive and above signal line
   - 30-day ROC is positive

2. **Sell when:**
   - Price falls below 120-day MA
   - 30-day MA crosses below 120-day MA (death cross)
   - MACD turns negative
   - 30-day ROC becomes negative

### Aggressive Approach (Short-term Traders)
1. **Buy when:**
   - Price crosses above 7-day MA
   - MACD histogram turns positive
   - 7-day ROC is positive and increasing
   - Price is above 30-day MA (confirms trend)

2. **Sell when:**
   - Price crosses below 7-day MA
   - MACD histogram turns negative
   - 7-day ROC becomes negative
   - Price falls below 30-day MA

### Momentum Confirmation
- **Strong Buy**: Multiple indicators align (price > all MAs, MACD bullish, positive ROC)
- **Strong Sell**: Multiple indicators align (price < all MAs, MACD bearish, negative ROC)
- **Wait**: Conflicting signals - wait for clearer trend

## Important Notes

- **No indicator is 100% accurate** - use multiple indicators together
- **Consider volume** - Price movements with high trading volume are more reliable
- **Market context matters** - External factors (new releases, market sentiment) can override technical signals
- **Risk management** - Always set stop-losses and take-profit targets
- **Backtesting** - Test strategies on historical data before committing real capital

## Indicator Availability

- **7-day MA**: Available after 7 days of data
- **30-day MA**: Available after 30 days of data
- **120-day MA**: Available after 120 days of data
- **MACD**: Available after 34 days of data (26 + 9 - 1)
- **ROC**: Available after the specified period (7 or 30 days)
